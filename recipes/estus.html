Estus

Description

Estus is arguably the most precious object that the Undead carry on their person. Filled on the bonfires that offer respite to this wretched souls, Estus is capable of healing every type of injury and keep any illness at bay while reinvigorating the mind and body of those who are lucky enough to carry it. So unless you are already dead there's no excuse to not make some Estus for yourself, because whether you need to slay a forgotten deity of yore or just get juiced up during those boring office hours, Estus got your back.


Ingredients

  • 1 estus flask (glass bottle for serving)
  • 6 ounces of pulp-free orange juice
  • 6 ounces of ginger beer
  • 2 shots of cinnamon whiskey
  • 3 ice cubes
  • Squeeze of lemon

Steps

  1. Blend orange juice, lemon juice, ice, and whiskey in a blender.
  2. Pour mixture into the serving bottle. Use a funnel if needed.
  3. Top off with ginger beer and try not to die out there.
